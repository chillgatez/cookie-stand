Pat's Salmon Cookies

Pat's is a family run business. From design to delivery Pat's family provides services and support to provide customer's with a one of a kind experience. They work to make Pat's dream a reality. By combining his love for sweets and his passion for healthy ecosystems. Pat has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection called Salmon Cookies. These cookies made into the shape of a salmon and are suitable for both humans and salmon to enjoy!
